HOW TO MINT MOOSH

STEP 1: GET A BITCOIN WALLET

IMPORTANT: SPARK PROTOCOL TOKENS

MOOSH tokens are LRC-20 tokens on Spark Protocol (Bitcoin L2). They require special Spark-compatible wallets, NOT regular Bitcoin wallets!

You need a Spark Protocol compatible wallet to receive MOOSH tokens:

RECOMMENDED

Spark Protocol Wallet

Official Spark Protocol wallet
Native LRC-20 support
Bitcoin L2 integration
Web-based interface

ACCESS SPARK WALLET

IMPORTANT NOTES:

  • Regular Bitcoin wallets (Exodus, Electrum, etc.) CANNOT receive MOOSH tokens
  • You must use Spark Protocol compatible wallets only
  • Tokens sent to wrong wallets may be permanently lost
  • Always verify wallet compatibility before minting

STEP 2: BUY BITCOIN FOR PAYMENT

You need Bitcoin (BTC) to PAY for minting MOOSH tokens. The BTC is used as payment - you'll receive MOOSH tokens in your Spark wallet:

HOW MUCH BITCOIN DO I NEED?

1 MOOSH = 0.0000058 BTC
1,000 MOOSH = 0.0058 BTC (~$290)

STEP 3: GET BITCOIN FOR PAYMENT

You need Bitcoin ready to send as payment. You can either use your exchange wallet or transfer to a personal Bitcoin wallet:

1

Open your wallet (Exodus, Electrum, etc.) and find your Bitcoin receiving address

2

Copy your Bitcoin address (starts with 1, 3, or bc1)

3

Go to your exchange (Coinbase/Binance) and withdraw Bitcoin to your address

4

Wait 10-60 minutes for the Bitcoin network to confirm the transaction

STEP 4: MINT YOUR MOOSH TOKENS

Now you're ready to mint MOOSH tokens!

1

Scroll down to the "MINT MOOSH" section on this page

2

Choose how many MOOSH tokens you want to mint (1 to 50,000)

3

Click "GENERATE PAYMENT" to see the exact Bitcoin amount and address

4

Send the EXACT Bitcoin amount from your wallet to our address

5

Your MOOSH tokens will be sent to your Spark Protocol wallet after payment confirmation!

IMPORTANT SECURITY TIPS

Never share your wallet seed phrase with anyone

Double-check all Bitcoin addresses before sending

Start with a small test amount first if you're new

Only use official wallet websites (avoid fake apps)
